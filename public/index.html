Thomas Counsell

Hello. I'm an engineer. At the moment I run the bit of the Department of Energy and Climate Change's strategy team that worries about the long-term. Before that I was an assistant to David MacKay, the department's Chief Scientific Advisor.

For the first year of my time at DECC I was seconded from the Carbon Trust. I worked for them from October 2007 in the Insights team, which meant I worked on publications that they produced on tricky climate change topics and helped them figure out how to improve what the Carbon Trust did.

Before that I was studying for a PhD at Clare College, Cambridge University. My research was with the Production Processes Group of the Manufacturing Division of the Engineering Department. My supervisor was Julian Allwood. I was also the teaching assistant on the Manufacturing Engineering Tripos course.
